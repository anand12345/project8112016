About Us

FOOD SAFETY AND QUALITY IS OUR BIGGEST FOCUS

At PV Sons, food safety and quality is critical and we don't compromise on any detail.

Infrastructure : We believe in focusing on our infrastructure so we can guarantee a finished product that is 100% safe and of the highest quality. Our building is built to match the most rigorous food safety standards with no gaps or open areas, thus ensuring a pest-free environment. The entire facility is constantly monitored and disinfected every 6 months by heating it to 50°-60°C.

Employee training : All our employees are trained to follow the strictest hygiene and food safety practices. We provide them with clean uniforms and other safety and hygiene gear like head nets, beard-nets, steel-toe shoes,etc. We also train employees to handle machines optimally and they are made aware of the occupational hazards and safety procedures.

Certification : At PV Sons, we take pride in our ‘A+’ BRC certification’ - a globally recognized standard in the food industry. We are always prepared for random external audits that are often carried out to ensure our plant adheres to strict safety and hygiene procedures. We are also Halal certified.

Raw material

We source our grain from the finest local farmers in Maharashtra, Telangana and Karnataka who are known for their superior quality of corn crop. In a pioneering move in India, we use our 7-fleet truck, to transfer the corn in loose form to the plant, thus eliminating dust and minimizing human touch. At PV Sons, we also believe in educating our suppliers on the finest farm practices as well as the latest know-how to ensure that the raw material is always of the highest quality and non-genetically modified.

Testing

In this stage, we test the samples of corn in our in~house laboratory for the level of Alfatoxin. This is naturally present in the crop and can be carcinogenic beyond a certain level. Only the crop that meets the permissible level is unloaded. If the percentage of Alfatoxin is high, we reject the entire batch.

Cleaning

The approved batch is unloaded and taken up for cleaning. It is cleaned to eliminate any dust, metal or corn cobs and is then moved to SlLOs for storage.

Storage

We store our grains in 2 large SlLOs that have a capacity of 5000 metric tonnes each. The grains can be stored for up to 6 months without losing its freshness. We follow a process called grain chilling to ensure that the grain is constantly stored at less than 15°C to keep it in good quality. A smaller SILO that can store 300 metric tonnes of grain is present for daily use.

Grading and Moisture Testing

The corn now moves in to the food processing plant where it is graded in 2 sizes: - 8mm — 4mm - 12mm — 8mm.ln Moisture Testing, we check the corn online continuously for the level of moisture. The ideal level of 13.5% is maintained to create a finished product that is always consistent in quality. At every level, the corn is cleaned and finer impurities are discarded. Magnets fixed attract any metal impurities that may be present.

Milling process

In this stage, we separate the corn into husk, germ and endosperm and it is then turned into finished products of various sizes and supplied in 25kg to 1-tonne bags. We store the finished product in clean,hygienic warehouses. Flaking Grits are run through a colour sorter, magnets and metal detector to remove any damaged grain and other impurities.

Finished product testing

We test the finished product across various factors like moisture content, Alfatoxin level, particle size and fat content before we dispatch it.This helps us deliver a high-grade and consistent raw material to our clients.

Traceability

Each batch of corn that is approved for unloading, is tagged with its area of produce. This tag is attached to that batch through all processes and until the finished product is packed. At any given point a batch of corn can be traced to its origin through the robust system set-up.
